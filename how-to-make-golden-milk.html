How to Make Golden Milk

How to make golden milk:
one cup raw milk or coconut milk, one tsp turmeric, pinch of black pepper, sea salt, cinnamon, and ginger, and sweetener to taste. Optional: you can add 1/2 tsp of ghee (for raw milk) or coconut oil (for coconut milk). Bring all the ingredients to a low simmer, not a boil, for 5-10 minutes, then enjoy hot or warm.
