How to Make Golden Milk

How to make golden milk (two ways): one cup raw milk or coconut milk, one tsp turmeric, pinch of black pepper, sea salt, cinnamon, and ginger, and sweetener to taste. Optional: you can add 1/2 tsp of ghee (for raw milk) or coconut oil (for coconut milk). Bring all the ingredients to a low simmer, not a boil, for 5-10 minutes, then enjoy hot or warm.

How to Make Golden Milk (Two Ways)

Golden Milk Elixir

A soothing, anti-inflammatory tonic crafted to support your wellness routine. Choose the traditional rich dairy version or a smooth plant-based alternative.

Step 1: Choose Your Variation

Option A: Rich & Traditional

  • 1 cup Raw Milk
  • Sweetener (to taste)
  • Optional: ½ tsp Ghee (for deep nourishment)

Option B: Plant-Based & Creamy

  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • Sweetener (to taste)
  • Optional: ½ tsp Coconut Oil (for healthy fats)

Step 2: Add Your Spice Core

Combine with your liquid base: 1 tsp Turmeric, a pinch of Black Pepper, Sea Salt, Cinnamon, and Ginger.

Pro Tip: Feel free to customize your blend with other warm aromatics like Cloves, Cardamom, or Nutmeg!

Step 3: Simmer & Serve

  1. Whisk all ingredients together thoroughly in a small saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a low simmer over medium-low heat. Do not let it reach a rolling boil to protect the properties of the raw ingredients.
  3. Allow the flavors to infuse at a low simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, pour into your favorite mug, and enjoy hot or warm.
