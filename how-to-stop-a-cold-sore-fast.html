How to stop a cold sore fast


Feel a cold sore coming? If you are currently experiencing an outbreak or feel one coming and are looking for a quick fix before you begin the cure manual, you can use things you are likely to have lying around.

Apply ice to the area to cool down the tingling and slow down the sores. Once you've iced the area, apply some toothpaste to the area and leave on overnight. You can put a pimple patch or tiny band aid over the toothpaste if you don't want the toothpaste to be messy while you sleep or if you simply don't want anyone to ask questions. Doing this stops the breakout in its tracks. If you already have an existing breakout, you can still do the same, but with a bit more persistence since the sore is already there. You can also apply some pure tea tree oil diluted with a bit of coconut oil or olive oil. Please remember to wash your hands thoroughly with warm, soapy water before and after touching the affected area for at least 30 seconds.
