Privacy Policy

At Freedom Remedies, accessible from our website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that is collected and recorded by Freedom Remedies and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our dedicated support channels.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information to provide better services and digital products to our users. The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you for it.

  • Purchase Information: When you purchase an ebook or guide, we collect information necessary to process your transaction. This may include your name, email address, billing address, and payment confirmation details.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us directly via email or a support form, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, the contents of the message, and any attachments you may send.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Like most standard websites, we automatically collect certain technical details when you browse our pages, including internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and optimize our Website and Services.
  • Process your transactions, verify your access, and securely deliver your digital ebook purchases.
  • Understand and analyze how you interact with our articles, guides, and resources to improve user experience.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, to provide customer support, transaction updates, and technical notifications.
  • Detect, prevent, and mitigate fraudulent transactions, unauthorized sharing of intellectual property, or malicious activity.

3. THIRD-PARTY PAYMENT PROCESSING (STRIPE)

All financial payments made on Freedom Remedies are handled and secured entirely by our third-party processing platform, **Stripe Checkout**. We do not collect, view, or store your credit card data, bank account numbers, or secondary financial logins on our systems.

Stripe's use of your personal information is governed by their respective privacy rules. You can review their data infrastructure policies directly on the Stripe Privacy Policy page.

4. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Freedom Remedies utilizes standard cookies to store essential information. These configurations are used to track visitor preferences, preserve your user state, and capture optimization analytics. This information allows us to customize our webpage contents based on your browser type or secondary device parameters to improve efficiency.

5. SECURITY OF YOUR DATA

The security of your personal data is highly important to us. Because we distribute our web layout using modern technical environments via GitHub and Cloudflare edge encryption, all communication routes between your browser and our platform use secure sockets layer technology (HTTPS). However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is completely error-free or impenetrable.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & ANALYTICS

This Privacy Policy does not apply to other external advertisers or third-party web platforms. We advise you to consult the respective privacy rules of these third-party systems for more detailed information. This may include their direct handling routines and instructions about how to opt-out of certain optimization trackers.

7. DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

We want to ensure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user interacting with our Services is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access — You have the right to request copies of the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to rectification — You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure — You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain legal conditions.
  • The right to data portability — You have the right to request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization or directly to you.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY PROTECTION

Another priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and monitor their children's online activity. Freedom Remedies does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think your child provided this kind of information on our site, please contact us immediately and we will remove it promptly.

9. GOVERNING LAW

This Privacy Policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Maryland, United States. Any enforcement, legal claims, or disputes regarding data practices must be brought exclusively in the appropriate regulatory frameworks located inside the State of Maryland.

10. CONTACT INFORMATION

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your data rights, or require assistance with your data records, please contact us via email at: support@yourdomain.com.
